Detailed Description

ASTVerifier verifies that various AST nodes can only be of a fixed range of subclasses.

These conditions cannot be enforced by a C++ compiler although the much the better. Example is the Target of an AssignStmt can only be a NameExpr or SubscriptExpr, not other things. The constrains are imposed by the language specification.
